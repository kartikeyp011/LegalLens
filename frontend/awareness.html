Fundamental Rights — Article 12–35

Part III of the Constitution of India

Article 14

Right to Equality

Equality before law and equal protection of laws within India.

Article 19

Right to Freedom

Freedom of speech, expression, assembly, movement, and profession.

Article 21

Right to Life

No person shall be deprived of life or personal liberty except by procedure established by law.

Article 22

Protection Against Arrest

Right to be informed of grounds of arrest, consult a lawyer, and produce before magistrate within 24 hours.

Article 32

Right to Constitutional Remedies

Right to move Supreme Court for enforcement of Fundamental Rights (Writ jurisdiction).

Article 39A

Free Legal Aid

State shall ensure equal justice and free legal aid to those who cannot afford it.
